State Legislator Survey

A non-partisan research study

What's holding back good policy?

As researchers, we want to know how to improve state policy and pass important legislation. To do so, we're asking for your help in understanding and solving the toughest policy bottlenecks facing state governments across the United States.

How to Participate

Please complete our 8-minute survey on state policy. In exchange, you can pick up your thank-you gift from our booth in the Exhibition Hall located on the Launchpad Pavilion (map).

Gifts range from a $5 gift card to prizes worth $25. We'll also email you a custom report on what voters in your district think about state policy.
